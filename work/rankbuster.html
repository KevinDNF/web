About

Rank busters is a game demo I created for my A-Level Computer Science coursework and the art assets where used on my Graphics Coursework. It's programmed in Monkey-X utilizing the mojo2 libraries.

The demo has been programmed and tested on Windows 7 and requires the Ted (Original Monkey-X IDE) to be installed for the audio to work.

Why Monkey-X ?

Monkey-X was not the best programming language for a game of this scale so why did I used it? Simple answer. Our teacher at the time told us to use it. His reasoning was that the language was so obscure that the examiners would have no clue how it works and would struggle marking the coursework resulting in higher grades.

Gameplay

The game was meant to be a Smash Bros copy-cat with original characters but similar gameplay. Due to time limitations I was only able to design 2 characters and implement 1 with a very basic attack.

Graphics

I was heavily inspired by The Worlds Ends with You NDS game.

Characters

The characters would each use ordinary items that have different abilities to attack.

Jennid would use her extensible scarf to grab opponents or just hit them.

Nor would use his pen to throw ink to his enemies and draw other items he could interact with.

Side notes

I encontered some limitations due to the fact that Monkey-X was still in development and some things did not work out of the box forcing me to find weird workarounds. The whole experience was really fun even if the end result didn't end up as I wanted it to be.

Monkey-X got later updated to Monkey2 and is still in development. I would love to port it to Monkey2 or a more developed language so that I can finish the game at some point.
