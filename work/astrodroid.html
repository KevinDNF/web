About

Astrodroid is a game I developed for a python competition at University of Portsmouth. The competition had heavy limitiations in which we could not use Classes or libraries other that zelle's Graphics.py.

We also had to base it on one of the two games the lecturer provided us.

Plan

I decided to create a platformer game that would utilize the floating mechanics of the "Android Chasing Apples" game and normal platformer mechanics.

The player would control an android with a jetpack that can be toggled on or off to change from normal platformer mechanics to floating mechanics. But to make it harder once the jetpack is on it would keep going upwards until toggled off.

I was going to use what I have learned from making Rank Busters to generate maps from files to create a maze in which the android had to avoid spikes to get to the apples or could just go to the exit with a lower score.

My main focus was gameplay as I didn't wanted to have nice looking graphics without good gameplay to go with it (rankbusters...).
